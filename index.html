About Me

I am an Associate Research Fellow at the Faculty of Computing, Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), where I work in close collaboration with Prof. Weizhe Zhang. I am also a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), under the supervision of Prof. Haibo Hu.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the School of Computer Science and Engineering and the ACT Institute at Beihang University in 2024. I have published more than ten papers in leading security and database venues, including TKDE, TIFS, TDSC, and ICDE.

Agent Security

Security and privacy across agent interaction, memory, tool use, and multi-agent collaboration.

Data Security

Security and privacy throughout data collection, storage, analysis, sharing, and open access.

Mentorship Philosophy

I am currently seeking self-motivated students and interns interested in agent security and data security. I independently recruit master's students, while prospective Ph.D. students may be jointly recruited and supervised with Prof. Weizhe Zhang and Prof. Hui He. Our goal is to pursue impactful research while helping students build strong foundations in research, academic writing, and systems development. Please feel free to contact me by email at any time.
